My Background

I started programming as a kid in my parents' basement using QBASIC and a DOS command prompt. In high school and college I started building web pages for fun and extra income. In college I studied Computer Science for two years, then graduated with a degree in Communication and a self-designed minor that includes classes in Business, Graphic Design and Digital Animation.

Since college, my career has included a wide variety of experience in full stack development, design, UX, project management, community organization, photography, videography, communication, writing, marketing, and SEO.

For the last several years I've been working remotely as a Full Stack Engineer and living as a nomad in over 20 countries. I love using technology to bring people and cultures together from around the world. I'm fluent in Spanish, I know the basics of Italian and I'm trying to pick up the basics of a few other languages.

After a short time at NASA helping to modernize an app that supports global climate science and disaster response, I'm working remotely and living a nomadic lifestyle again.
